Welcome to my Academic Site

Currently, I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of Groningen.

I dedicate my research to (1) understanding how individuals engage in decision-making, (2) making sense of complex social phenomena, and (3) developing methods that will help us answer causal questions with observational data (that is to say, almost all data that exist).
