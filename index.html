jb.louw(at)maastrichtuniversity.nl

My mission is to help decision-makers get the most out of their data. My research combines econometrics and machine learning. The aim is to establish a suite of automated algorithms that can provide causal insights that are actionable instead of correlational insights that are merely informative. I am part of the team that organize the Causal Data Science Meeting.
