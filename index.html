I am a PhD Candidate in Strategy and Causal Inference. My interest lies in the intersection of econometrics, causal inference and machine learning. My research apply these models to solve challenges in strategy and innovation. I am also on the organizing committee for the Causal Data Science Meeting , an annual meeting that unites researchers and data scientists from academia and industry to build on ideas of causal inference in data science. Reach out if you have want to know more about the meeting.
